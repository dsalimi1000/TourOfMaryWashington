University of Mary Washington
*****
Locations:
Double Drive
Welcome to the University of Mary Washington! You are
currently at the main entrance for the school.
+++
James Farmer Hall
This building is named after Dr. James Farmer and houses
the Departments of Mathematics, Computer Science, CPR,
Education and Asian Studies. It was built in 1941 and was
originally the library. An addition was completed in 1963.
+++
Campus Walk
This sidewalk winds through the entire campus leading
you to all other locations on campus.
+++
Bell Tower
The tower houses a clock and is a monumental piece at UMW
built in 2007. The bell chimes twice a day at noon and 6pm.
+++
George Washington Hall
This building has all administrators and human resources.
It was completed in 1939.
+++
Combs Hall
Combs Hall contains the Department of English and Linguistics.
It was built in 1959.
+++
Mason Hall 
This is a residence hall across from GW. It is connected to 
Randolph Hall through the Link.
+++
Randolph Hall 
This is a residence hall across from GW. It is connected to 
Mason Hall through the Link.
+++
Virginia Hall 
This is the second oldest residence hall on campus. It was completed 
in 1915. It has a lovely parlour off the lobby.
+++
Lee Hall 
Lee Hall houses the bookstore, student services and the health center.
+++
Ball Circle
This is a nice open space for students to relax, study outside, hang out with friends and 
host events on campus. On a warm day you are likely to see students throwing a frisbee!
+++
University Center 
This is the newest building on campus being built in 2015. It has the main dining hall, 
the James Farmer Multicultural Center (JFMC), the Community Outreach and Resources Center (COAR) 
and many others.
+++
The Link
The Link is the section that connects Randolph and Mason Halls and is also a student residence.  
+++
